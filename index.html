Visual First Aid Guide — CPR

Follow these 8 steps in a cardiac emergency. Call 911 first.

1 Check Safety
2 Check Response
3 Call 911
4 Open Airway
5 Check Breathing
6 30 Compressions
7 2 Rescue Breaths
8 Repeat 30:2
Hands-only CPR: Not trained in rescue breaths? Skip step 7. Compressions alone still save lives.
